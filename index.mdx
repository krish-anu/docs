## What is Trash2Treasure?

Trash2Treasure is Sri Lanka's leading digital waste aggregation and recycling marketplace. Turn your waste into cash by scheduling pickups, tracking impact, and getting paid instantly through mobile wallets or bank transfers.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a Pickup">
    Select waste type, estimate weight, and schedule your pickup window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We Collect & Sort">
    Our verified recyclers collect, weigh, and sort responsibly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Paid Instantly">
    Receive money instantly to your mobile wallet or bank account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

- **Verified Recyclers** - We partner only with certified recycling facilities that meet environmental standards.
- **Transparent Pricing** - Real-time pricing updates based on market rates. Final price confirmed after inspection.
- **Carbon Tracking** - Track your personal contribution to reducing CO₂ emissions with every collection.
- **Community Driven** - Building a sustainable future together with local communities across Sri Lanka.
- **User Authentication** - Secure login with JWT and OTP verification
- **Booking Management** - Create, track, and manage waste pickup bookings
- **Real-time Notifications** - Push notifications for booking updates
- **Admin Dashboard** - Comprehensive admin panel for platform management
- **Driver App** - Dedicated interface for drivers to manage pickups
- **Pricing Management** - Dynamic pricing based on waste categories
- **Chat Support** - Real-time chat system for user communication
